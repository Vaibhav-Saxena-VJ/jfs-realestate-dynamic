Lodha Belmondo

At Mumbai-Pune Expressway, Pune

Lodha Belmondo sets a new standard for luxury living in Pune. Spanning across 108 acres of meticulously landscaped havens, its 48 elegant buildings offer panoramic city views and spacious sanctuaries designed for discerning tastes, ranging from 2/3 BHK. Open courtyards and lush gardens create a vibrant community atmosphere, while the project boasts an impressive 90 acres of open greens and a sprawling 45-acre golf course, ensuring a serene and luxurious lifestyle for its residents.

Within the 100-acre luxury resort of Lodha Belmondo, on the banks of the Pavana river, is The Reserve, an exclusive gated enclave of spacious 4-bed apartments, villas and country houses set around a stunning golf course.
